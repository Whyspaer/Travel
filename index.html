Myakka River State Park

One of the many wonders of the Myakka River State Park is the manatee.

Of the many species of animals found in Florida, the alligator can be found here too. Aliigators are one the oldest known reptiles.

Of the many things to do in Myakka River state park, camping is one of the favorite things to do. Whether it be just off the trail or primitive camping
